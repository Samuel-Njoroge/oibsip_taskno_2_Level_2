GUIDO VAN ROSSUM.

The father of the Python Programming Language.








Early life and Education.

  • 1. Guido van Rossum was born in 31/01/1956 in Netherlands with his brother Just van Rossum, who is a type designer and programmer who designed the typeface used in the "Python Powered" logo.
  • 2. He received a master's degree in Mathematics and Computer Science from the University of Amsterdam in 1982.
  • Python.

    In December 1989, he had been looking for a 'hobby' programming project that would keep him occupied during the week around Christmas as his office was closed when he decided to write an interpreter for a new scripting language with a descendant of ABC that would appeal to Unix/C hackers. He chose the name "Python"meaning being slightly in a irreverent mood and a big fan of Monty Python's Flying Circus.

Work and Major Contributions.

  • Computer Programming for Everybody Proposal.
    In 1999 , he submitted the funding proposal to DARPA in which he defined his goals for Python. In 2019, Python became the second most popular language on GitHub.
  • Google.
    At Google, he developed Mondrian, a web-based code review system written in Python and used within the company.
  • Dropbox.
    In 2013, he started working at the cloud file storage company Dropbox.
  • Microsoft.
    In 12 November 2020 he joined the Developer Division at Microsoft.
